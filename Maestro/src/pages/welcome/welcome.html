Welcome to Maestro

This application is designed to speed up complex skill development by increasing your learning efficiency.

You will first start by completing a survey that will give you a better idea of what type of learner you are.

The survey will consist of three section

  1. Visual

  2. Auditory

  3. Kinesthetic

After completion of this survey, you will be given some information about your learning preferences as well as learning tips for each category
